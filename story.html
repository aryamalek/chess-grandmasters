The Grandmasters

In 1950, FIDE created the Grandmaster title and handed it to 27 living players at once. Every one since has earned it. Scroll to watch the world's chess elite take shape, one title at a time.

Data: FIDE grandmaster titles, 1950–present. Dissolved federations (Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Yugoslavia, East/West Germany) are shown under their principal present-day successor state.
